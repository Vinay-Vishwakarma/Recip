Recipe

Choclate cake

Here is a fast easy version of classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

Suger: 150g

Flour: 80g

Egg: 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and th butter together

2

Mix the suger, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
